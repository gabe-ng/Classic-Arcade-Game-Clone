Udacity Arcade Game Clone

OH NO!

Your friends have been trapped on an island surrounded by sharks! Navigate your way through the sea to rescue your friends!

Instructions

Use your arrow keys to cross the ocean. Avoid the sharks at all costs! Once you make it to the other side, one of your friends will be brought to safety, but you must make it back for the rest!

Note

The player is safe to touch the upper tip of the shark's fin that extends into the row above!
